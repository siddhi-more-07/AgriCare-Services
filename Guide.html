Quick introduction about the platform

"Our platform provides a complete range of farming essentials, including fertilizers and a variety of equipment such as tractors, plows, harvesters, cultivators, planters, seeders, sprinklers, combine harvesters, mowers, and trailers. In addition to offering these products, we facilitate direct selling opportunities for farmers to market their produce, which includes fruits, pulses, vegetables, flowers, and more, ensuring fair prices by eliminating middlemen. We also assist farmers in diversifying their income streams by helping them transform parts of their farms into tourist attractions. Our dedicated team collaborates with farmers through our website, offering consultations and services for farm decoration aimed at enhancing recognition and revenue from tourism. Furthermore, we have a team of experienced doctors available online to aid farmers in identifying and addressing plant and animal diseases. Farmers can conveniently book appointments for consultations and receive professional guidance and assistance. Our platform also supports farmers who prefer renting equipment instead of making direct purchases. Users can create customized profiles to track their activities within the system, enjoying benefits such as real-time updates, the ability to post services, monitor posted services, receive and manage booking requests, connect directly with customers, review profile history for changes and improvements, and track sold services."
