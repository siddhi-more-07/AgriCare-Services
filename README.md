# AgriCare-Services
One stop Shop for all Agricultural Services.

The platform offers everything farmers need for farming, like fertilizers, equipment such as tractors, plows, harvesters, cultivators, planters, seeders, sprinklers, combine harvesters, mowers, and trailers. Additionally, farmers can sell their produce—fruits, pulses, vegetables, flowers, and more—at fair prices without middlemen.

We also provide services to help farmers turn parts of their farms into tourist attractions. They can contact our team through our website to discuss farm decoration for tourism, boosting their recognition and income.

Moreover, we have a team of doctors available online who can identify plant and animal diseases. Farmers can book appointments with them for consultations and assistance when needed.

Additionally, Farmers have a range of profile features at their fingertips to manage their online presence and connect with customers effectively. They can easily update their profile details, view their profile history, post their services, track the performance of their offerings, check for booked services, and connect directly with customers. These features empower farmers to maintain accurate and up-to-date profiles.
